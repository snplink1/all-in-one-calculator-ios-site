Overview

All-in-One Calculator is designed as a privacy-friendly calculator app. The app provides basic calculator, scientific calculator, unit converter, GST calculator, EMI calculator, and age calculator features.

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. Calculations are performed locally on your device.

Information We Collect

The app does not collect personal information. It does not require account creation, login, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment details.

Local Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store limited preferences locally on your device, such as selected theme, recent calculations, or calculation history. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

You can clear available history inside the app, or remove all locally stored app data by deleting the app.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties. The app does not include advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party analytics services.

Internet Access

The current version of All-in-One Calculator does not require internet access for its core calculator features. All calculations are handled locally on the device.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality changes. The updated date on this page will automatically reflect the latest page update date when hosted.
